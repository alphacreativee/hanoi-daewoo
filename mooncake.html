2026 Mid-Autumn Festival

The year 2026 marks a remarkable milestone as Hanoi Daewoo Hotel celebrates 30 years of hospitality excellence and culinary prestige in the heart of the capital. To honor this enduring legacy, Hanoi Daewoo Hotel proudly presents Moonlit Magnolia - an exclusive Mid-Autumn collection designed to convey heartfelt wishes of harmony, prosperity, and happiness to those who matter most.

From the elegant Silver box to the luxurious Diamond box, every detail has been thoughtfully curated to transform each gift into a meaningful gesture of appreciation. More than a seasonal gift, Moonlit Magnolia is a tribute to reunion, gratitude, and the bonds that endure through time.

Indoor Venues

Our indoor venues include the Grand Ballroom and several smaller rooms. The Grand Ballroom features modern architecture and can host up to 420 guests. It's perfect for large banquets, corporate events, or wedding receptions.

Outdoor Venues

With a backdrop of lush greenery, the Serenity Poolside venue at the Hanoi Daewoo Hotel offers a sophisticated outdoor space for your wedding, corporate retreat, or social gathering. Celebrate with up to 300 guests in style.
