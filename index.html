Welcome to Hanoi Daewoo Hotel

Being at the forefront of upscale hospitality in Hanoi since 1996, Hanoi Daewoo Hotel has been the preferred address for visiting dignitaries and heads of state for over three decades. Nestled in a prestigious neighborhood surrounded by embassies and parks, the hotel offers convenient access to the city’s key business and cultural destinations.

Designed in a timeless classic style, Hanoi Daewoo continues its legacy of legendary hospitality for business and leisure travelers worldwide. With state-of-the-art MICE facilities and elegant function spaces, Hanoi Daewoo is also the preferred venue in Hanoi for weddings, conferences and international events.

Featuring well-appointed accommodations, distinctive dining experiences, indulgent wellness facilities, picturesque gardens, and the largest hotel swimming pool in the city, Hanoi Daewoo Hotel promises an exceptional stay, no matter the purpose of your visit.
