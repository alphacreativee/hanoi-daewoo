Floral Reverie - 2025 Mooncake Collection

Honoring nearly three decades of celebrating the Mid-Autumn artistry, Hanoi Daewoo Hotel unveils its 2025 mooncake collection Hoa Ca – Floral Reverie. A luminous fusion of color, scent, and aroma, this exquisite creation brings to life a dreamlike garden of reunion and joy.

Infused with a spirit of wanderlust and modern elegance, Lunar Bloom embodies a fresh take on festive gifting through its fashion-forward, handbag-inspired design, intricate embossed floral patterns, and a vibrant interplay of colors. A brand-new creation for this year’s Mid-Autumn celebration, Sakura Dream draws inspiration from the delicate beauty of cherry blossoms beneath a golden moon. With its slender silhouette, polished metallic clasp, and refined leather handle, the design evokes the charm of a timeless vanity case.

Through exquisite ingredients, delicate flavors, and exceptional design, the Floral Reverie collection celebrates Hanoi Daewoo Hotel’s heritage of craftsmanship and refinement in luxury mooncakes. More than a festive indulgence, each elegant gift box is a refined expression of gratitude for valued partners, clients, and cherished loved ones.

Hanoi Daewoo Hotel's 2025 mooncake collection is available at the hotel lobby and Brodard Gourmet from 08/08/2025.

Special Offers:

  • Early Bird: 20% discount on purchase with full payment before 28/08/2025.
  • 10% discount for Daewoo Preferred Card holders.
  • Up to 30% discount for bulk purchases.
  • Free delivery to one address for purchase of 30 boxes and above within 15km from the hotel.
